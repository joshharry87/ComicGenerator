# Vision

**Project:** Comic Generator  
**Status:** Pre-Alpha / Planning  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Last Updated:** 02 July 2026

## Vision statement

Comic Generator is an open-source project with the aim to fascilitate generating comics and simple 2 Dimensional animation representations of input information, specifically targetting making more digestible content out of scientific literature, news articles and other professional mediums. 

Comic Generator exists to improve access to real world information through thoughtful and fun entertainment generation, aiming to betranslate tough concepts into story or narrative driven information. The overall goal is to improve access to education for all age groups and learning levels. 

## Mission

To develop a modern, open-source Algorithmic or AI driven tool for converting multiple perspective imagery to comics and or 2D animation, with naturla language generated story lines based on real world research and news.

## Product thesis

News and research are often uninteresting or unpalletable to a large section of the community, this hopes to provide more equal access to more people, similar products exist but are more often information dumps or overly childish which results in a less consumable or unideal solution to.

Comic Generator should feel easy to use and provide users the ability to use their own art styles and narrative styles applied to the generation process, to allow for a broader consumer market for more topics.

## Target outcome

Comic generator should become a reliable application that:

- is easy to open and navigate at the interface level
- allow for uploading multiple view points of users own IP art styles for generation of characters scenes and frames.
- provide a broad range of scene options, preferably sourced from real world location based imagery.
- provide easily convertible information to narrative for review before comic generation.
- Provide for eassy narrative to comic interface and generation.
- Provide easy narrative to simple short animation for shorter form bits of narrative.
- Explains limitations honestly.
- Remains maintainable for contributors.

## Objectives

Comic Generator's initial objectives are:

1. Build a tool that allows users to upload 4 view angles of the relevant character images.
2. Convert those to a repository for the users to generate more characters based off their styled uploads.
3. Allow generation of characters to be stored.
4. Allow reuse of characters for the different mediums for the user.
5. Allow generation of a simple 4 to 12 frame comic based off of input text.
6. Allow for conversion of text information into a narrative format, presented for the user to review before use in comic conversion.
7. Allow for users to customise the style of narrative format, either through training or through interface controls.


## Design philosophy

Comic Generator's interface is designed around the belief that software should serve the user rather than compete for the user's attention.

Features should exist because they improve usability, clarity, or reliability. Features should not be added because they are technically interesting, commonly found elsewhere, or likely to increase engagement metrics.

Where multiple solutions exist, preference should be given to the simplest solution that satisfies the project's documented objectives and constraints.

## Version 1 vision

Version 1 focuses on compatibility with Reddit and on establishing a stable, maintainable foundation. The goal is not to reproduce every Reddit feature or every feature of existing clients. The goal is to deliver a polished foundation that proves the core experience:

- Browse communities.
- Browse posts.
- Read comments.
- Manage local favourites.
- Search where supported.
- Configure basic local settings.
- Understand privacy and platform limitations.

## Long-term direction

Comic Generator may later support additional capabilities depending on the outcomes from the MVA.


## Success criteria

Comic Generator will be considered successful when it provides users with:

- A clean and responsive interface.
- A browsing experience that feels natural and intuitive.
- Most of the target criteria are met with a 80% user acceptance in work flow.
